Primevideo.com/mytv



Primevideo.com/mytv is a web portal created by Amazon to help users link their devices to their Prime Video accounts. Whether you are using a smart TV, gaming console, streaming stick, or other supported devices, this portal simplifies the process of accessing Amazon Prime Video content. When you first set up Prime Video on a new device, you will often be prompted to visit Primevideo.com/mytv. This is where you can enter a unique code generated by your device to authenticate it with your Amazon account.


How to use Prime Video?

  • Step 1: Launch Prime Video on Your Device
    • Open the Prime Video app on your compatible device (like your smart TV, streaming device, or gaming console).
    • Look for your account or a ‘Sign In’ option. Upon selection, a unique activation code will typically be displayed on your screen.
  • Step 2: Visit PrimeVideo.com/MyTV
    • Using a web browser on your computer or mobile device, go to Primevideo.com/mytv.
    • You’ll see a prompt asking for the activation code that was displayed on your device.
  • Step 3: Enter the Activation Code
    • Enter the code in the provided field and click on the “Continue” or “Submit” button.
    • If you aren’t already logged into your Amazon account, you may need to do so at this stage.
  • Step 4: Complete the Activation
    • Once entered, your device will automatically register with your Amazon Prime Video account.
    • You should receive a confirmation message, and after a brief moment, you’ll be able to access all available content on your device.

Install Prime Video on Your Devices

Smart TVs:

  • Go to your TV's app store (e.g., Samsung Apps, LG Content Store).
  • Search for "Amazon Prime Video".
  • Select the app and click "Install".
  • Launch the app and sign in with your Amazon account.

Streaming Devices:

  • Go to your device's app store (e.g., Roku Channel Store, Apple App Store).
  • Search for "Amazon Prime Video".
  • Select the app and click "Add Channel" or "Install".
  • Launch the app and sign in with your Amazon account.

Mobile Devices:

  • Go to your device's app store (e.g., Google Play Store, Apple App Store).
  • Search for "Amazon Prime Video".
  • Select the app and click "Install".
  • Launch the app and sign in with your Amazon account.

How to activate your Amazon Prime Video device at Primevideo.com/mytv?

  • Open your web browser and navigate to primevideo.com/mytv.
  • Sign in with your Amazon account.
  • Type in the code displayed on your device (e.g., TV, streaming device, or mobile app).
  • After entering the code, follow any additional on-screen instructions to complete the activation process.
  • Check your device for confirmation of successful registration.
